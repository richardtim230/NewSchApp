  2. Go to Mock Tests to take weekly or semester-based exams.

  4. Earn points for every participation, which you can convert to cash or rewards.

For Marketplace Users: Safe Buying & Selling

  1. To Buy: Browse Marketplace, search or filter for items, and chat directly with the seller.
  2. To Sell: Click Post an Item, upload details and images, and wait for OAU students to reach out.
