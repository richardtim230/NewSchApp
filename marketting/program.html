International Baccalaureate (IBDP)

Two-year diploma designed for motivated students preparing for competitive international universities.

Program highlights

Subject breadth: Six subject groups plus Theory of Knowledge.
Extended Essay: Independent research project supervised by faculty.
CAS: Creativity, Activity & Service integrated into the student experience.
University guidance: Dedicated counsellors for application strategy and essay coaching.

Typical assessment

Final external exams, internal assessments, and an externally moderated diploma. Students receive full support with exam preparation.

Entry profile

Academic record, teacher references and interview. Some conditional offers for strong applicants.

Duration

Typically two years (Years 12–13).

Contact

Email admissions@examguard.edu | +33 1 23 45 67 89

Duration

French as a Foreign Language (FLE)

Flexible programs for different levels: intensive short courses, semester immersion or full-year integration.

Course features

Levels: A1 to C2; placement test on arrival.
Method: Communicative & task-based learning with cultural outings.
Assessment: Regular progress reviews and certificates.
Length

From 2-week intensives to full school year options.
