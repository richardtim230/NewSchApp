A welcoming residential community

Students live in comfortable houses supervised by experienced houseparents and tutors. Life at our boarding school balances structured routines with extracurricular choice so students can thrive academically and personally.

Boarding houses

Each house is a supportive family unit with its own character, dedicated pastoral staff and communal spaces for study and relaxation.

Oak House
A lively house with strong student leadership and evening study routines.
Elm House
Quieter boarding with a focus on independent study and arts.

Pine House

Pastoral care & wellbeing

Student wellbeing is central: trained houseparents, regular wellbeing check-ins, on-site medical support and tailored mentor programs help students settle and flourish.

Safeguarding

Strict safeguarding policies and trained staff ensure a safe environment day and night.

Counselling

Confidential counselling services and mental-health workshops for students and families.

Health & Medical

On-campus nurse and links with local healthcare providers for urgent and routine care.
