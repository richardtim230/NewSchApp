Overview

ExamGuard Int'l School is an academically rigorous and culturally diverse boarding school. We combine small class sizes, individualized coaching and a rich extracurricular program to develop confident, curious and resilient learners. Our pastoral care and residential life support students' wellbeing and growth.

History & milestones

1899
Founded as a local boarding institution focused on classical studies.
1978
Expanded with new science and sports facilities and international partnerships begin.
2007
Transition to a fully bilingual curriculum and strengthened university guidance.
2024
Invested in STEAM learning spaces and boarding house refurbishments.

Academic philosophy

Our teaching approach balances academic challenge with personalized support. We emphasize critical thinking, research-led learning and bilingual competence while providing pathways to global universities (IB, French Bac). Small classes and a 4:1 coaching ratio ensure each student receives mentoring tailored to their goals.

Visit & admissions

Book a campus visit or join an open day. Admissions includes a simple application, assessment and interview. We welcome enquiries from families worldwide and provide assistance with visas and boarding placement.
