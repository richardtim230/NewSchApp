Our mission & approach

We believe that academic excellence and pastoral care go hand in hand. Our curriculum blends rigorous subject teaching with personalised coaching, small tutorial groups and a broad co‑curricular offer — arts, sport and service learning — so students leave confident, curious and prepared for global citizenship.

The boarding environment is intentionally structured: tutors, houseparents and activity leaders work closely with families to ensure wellbeing, balanced routines and strong pastoral oversight.

History & milestones

1899
Founded as a progressive boarding school with an emphasis on student autonomy.
1950s–1980s
