Overview

ExamGuard International School blends a rich historical legacy with modern international curricula — including the International Baccalaureate and French Baccalaureate pathways — delivered in small classes with strong pastoral support. Our boarding community focuses on academic excellence, wellbeing and character development.

History & identity

Established in 1898 as a regional boarding institution, the School has progressively internationalised while preserving many traditions that define our culture and ceremonies. Over time we have invested in bilingual education, science & arts facilities, and pastoral systems to support a diverse student body.

Academic excellence

IB, French Baccalaureate and bespoke pathways prepare students for leading universities worldwide.

Pastoral care

House-based boarding, experienced tutors and structured wellbeing programmes for every student.

Global community

Students from 50+ nationalities, language immersion and international partnerships.

Facilities

Modern science labs, arts studios, competition-standard sports facilities and secure boarding houses.

Campus & facilities

Our purpose-maintained campus balances heritage buildings with modern learning spaces. Facilities include science and technology labs, an arts centre, library, music practice rooms, performance theatre, sports pitches and secure boarding houses.

Student life & co-curricular

An intentionally broad co-curricular programme supports student interests and aspirations: competitive sport, performing arts, community service and outdoor education. Leadership, wellbeing and service are embedded into our boarding routines.

Boarding houses

Homely, staffed houses with dedicated houseparents and structured pastoral care.

Arts & sport

High-quality studios, fields and coaching for a wide range of disciplines.

Global outlook

Exchange schemes and international collaborations broaden perspectives.
