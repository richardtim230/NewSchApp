How ExamGuard Rewards Students
ExamGuard motivates academic engagement by awarding credit points for:
  • Completing mock tests and simulation exams
  • Practicing past questions
  • Writing/reviewing articles and blog posts
Credit points are credited to each student's Study Wallet and can be:
  • Converted to withdrawable cash (bank/airtime payout)
  • Used in ExamGuard's online marketplace (books, gadgets, etc.)
This links diligence with real financial reward and support.
