Partner with ExamFund — Bank & Fintech Pilot

ExamFund combines an AI Study Partner, timed mock exams & analytics, a notes & tutoring marketplace, and a Study Wallet for sponsored micro‑grants. We launched with 2,000+ registrations (pilot activity at Obafemi Awolowo University) and are positioned to scale across Nigerian universities with the right partners.

Why partner (Banks & Fintech)

  • CSR impact: convert funds into non‑repayable micro‑grants that remove exam-time barriers.
  • Customer acquisition: co‑brand Study Wallet placements; pilot student savings / campus accounts.
  • Talent pipeline: run sponsored study challenges to identify top candidates for internships & graduate roles.
  • Measurable outcomes: aggregated KPI dashboards for reporting & audits.
