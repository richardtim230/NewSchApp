What is ExamGuard Nigeria?
ExamGuard Nigeria is a multi-institution digital platform focused on academic and financial support for tertiary students nationwide. Features include:
  • Smart Mock Exams & AI Study Partner: Weekly, semester-based, and field-specific evaluations with performance analytics and AI-powered tutor.
  • Digital Credit Points: Students earn points for practicing questions, taking mock tests, reading and reviewing educational blogs, and more.
  • Flexible Reward System: Points can be exchanged for withdrawable cash, or redeemed on the ExamGuard online marketplace for books, supplies, gadgets, and study essentials at subsidized rates.
  • Trusted Marketplace: Safe, student-focused buying/selling for verified students (no scams, privacy-respected).
  • Real Partner Impact: Micro-grants and scholarships are instantly distributed to eligible students, with transparent, anonymized reporting for partners, sponsors, fintechs and employers.

FAQ & Audience

Who can benefit? Nigerian Universities, Polytechnics, and Colleges of Education — any registered tertiary student or institution.
How are funds awarded? Via instant micro-grants using our Study Wallet; points earned through verified learning can be converted into cash or used for subsidized marketplace goods.
How is privacy handled? No PII is shared without consent; partners receive only anonymized, aggregate student engagement data.

FAQ & Ethics

How is student data handled?

No PII is shared without explicit consent. Impact reports use only aggregated, anonymized data.

Can sponsors direct funds to specific needs?

Yes — need-based, verified-activity, or open grants are all possible options.

What about misuse or cheating?

Only engagement for reporting; strong anti‑cheat and abuse controls in place.
