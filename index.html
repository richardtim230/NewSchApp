What is OAU ExamGuard?

OAU ExamGuard is a unit under ExamGuard Nigeria, established at OAU to support students both academically and financially. It provides essential academic resources for test and exam preparation, including real-time scheduled tests and mock exams. Financial support is offered by allowing users to write and post articles, blogs, or verified news, from which they can earn income. Students also earn credit points by participating in mock tests and exams—points are awarded based on scores and for completing academic-related tasks. These credit points can be used to purchase items in the OAU ExamGuard online marketplace or withdrawn as cash.

All these measures are designed to help students minimize financial stress and focus more on their studies.
